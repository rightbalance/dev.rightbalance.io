Senior QA Manual Engineer

Overview

We're looking for a Senior QA Manual Engineer to join our team. Headquartered in Los Angeles, California, Right Balance applies the latest technology and the best engineering practices to help businesses grow. We’re in the top 50 companies to watch in LA.

Project Details

Our client is a leading software solutions provider in the online advertising market. The platform streamlines the ad sales processes for some of the most prominent publishers on the Web.

We’re committed to continuous innovation by using the latest technologies in the cloud arena. We’re moving to microservices, optimizing the flow of data in the AdTech stack to connect publishers and advertisers in a meaningful way.

You'll be working with a large scale system involving a lot of features, working with terabytes of data in our database. Performance is important for our team, as well as scalability. Constantly challenging the status quo, we're always improving our processes. Your voice will be heard.

You'll make an impact on the team by utilizing your technological experience.

Our stack is .NET on the backend, moving to .NET Core with SQL Server deployed to Azure Cloud. On the frontend, we’re using Vue.js. Our release pipeline is streamlined with CI/CD running on Azure pipelines. For our infrastructure, we believe in managing it as code, currently using Terraform to do so. Some of our services use Azure functions, as well as running in containers on Kubernetes. Having a lot of fun doing it, join us!

What’s in it for you

1. Work with the latest and greatest technologies.

2. Learn from the best people. We constantly challenge the status quo, learn from each other, and invent new ways of building a great product.

3. We actually write automated tests. We take advantage of the latest automation tools like automatic deployments, CI/CD pipelines, and more.

4. Work remotely at the comfort of your home or a shared co-working space.

5. Work on challenging problems, innovate, have fun while doing it.

6. Travel around the world. We offer occasional business trips to work with our clients face to face from their offices.

Required Qualifications

1. 4+ years of professional full-time experience doing manual QA.

2. 2+ years of SQL experience.

3. Upper-intermediate to fluent speaking and writing English.

4. Full understanding of SDLC.

5. Full understanding of QA strategy.

6. Full understanding of the quality test cycle.

7. Experience creating and documenting test cases.

8. Strong analytical abilities.

9. Knowledge and understanding of automated testing and how it works together with the QA strategy.

10. Basic understanding of the software architecture.

11. Solid troubleshooting skills.

Nice to haves

1. A formal Computer Science or equivalent degree.

2. Zephyr experience.

3. CI/CD system experience (we use Azure Pipelines)

4. Cloud experience (preferably Azure)

5. API Testing experience with Postman (SOAP and Rest)

6. Selenium or equivalent experience

7. Software development familiarity.

Frequently Asked Questions

Most of our long-term full-time engagements last multiple years.
